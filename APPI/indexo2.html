Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our water delivery services

How quickly can you deliver water in an emergency?

For emergency situations in Accra, we guarantee delivery within 2-4 hours. We maintain a fleet of dedicated emergency response vehicles ready to deploy 24/7. Simply call our emergency hotline at +233 55 100 7133 and we'll dispatch immediately.

What quality standards does your water meet?

All our water meets or exceeds Ghana Water Company standards. We perform regular testing for purity, contaminants, and mineral content. Our industrial water meets specific industry requirements. Certificates of analysis are available upon request.

Do you offer scheduled delivery services?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduled delivery options including weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly service. We work with you to create a delivery schedule that matches your consumption patterns. Our automated reminder system ensures you never run out of water.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash on delivery. For commercial accounts, we offer monthly billing with approved credit. Online payments can be made through our secure payment portal.

Can I track my delivery in real-time?

Yes! All our delivery vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking. Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a text message with a link to track your delivery in real-time on a map. You'll also receive an ETA update 30 minutes before arrival.
